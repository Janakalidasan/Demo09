WHAT IS FOREX?

Foreign exchange, or Forex for short, is quite simply a market where you're able to exchange one currency for another. When
you 'sell' a currency, there is a buyer for that currency somewhere else. Now, the exchange rate between those two
currencies is what's important when trading forex. The exchange rate is constantly fluctuating, and it's these fluctuations
that allow market speculators to earn from trading..
