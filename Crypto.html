WHAT IS CRYPTO TRADING?

A cryptocurrency is a digital (virtual) financial currency, where security of making transactions is provided by using cryptography methods. The concept of the very first digital currency, Bitcoin, was introduced by Satoshi Nakamoto in 2008.
